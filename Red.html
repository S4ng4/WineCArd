Barolo DOCG 2018

Technical Details

Producer: Traditional Piedmont Winery

Region: Piedmont, Italy

Grapes: Nebbiolo 100%

Aging: 24 months in oak barrels

Alcohol: 14.5%

Serving Temperature: 18°C

Tasting Notes

Appearance: Deep garnet red with orange highlights

Nose: Intensely aromatic with notes of rose, tar, herbs, spices, and red fruits

Palate: Full-bodied, elegant and rich in tannins with a complex structure and long finish

Food Pairing: Perfect with braised meats, game, truffle dishes, and aged cheeses

Chianti Classico Riserva

Technical Details

Producer: Traditional Tuscan Estate

Region: Tuscany, Italy

Grapes: Sangiovese 90%, Canaiolo 10%

Aging: 24 months in Slavonian oak casks

Alcohol: 13.5%

Serving Temperature: 16-18°C

Tasting Notes

Appearance: Ruby red tending to garnet

Nose: Intense aromas of cherry, plum, leather, tobacco and slight hint of violet

Palate: Balanced and persistent with elegant tannins and good acidity

Food Pairing: Ideal with pasta al ragù, roasted lamb, grilled steak, and aged pecorino cheese

Ratti - Marcenasco 2021

Technical Details

Producer: Ratti

Region: DOCG Barolo, Piedmont, Italy

Grapes: Nebbiolo 100%

Vintage: 2021

Aging: 24 months in Slavonian oak

Alcohol: 14.5%

Serving Temperature: 18°C

Cellaring Potential: 2026-2043

Tasting Notes

Visual: Luminous garnet with orange rim

Nose: Rose petal, wild cherry, licorice, crushed mint

Palate: Structured yet refined, firm tannins, vibrant acidity, saline finish

Vintage Notes: Balanced vintage with warm days and cool nights, ideal for Nebbiolo's aromatic complexity

Food Pairing: Tajarin pasta with truffles, braised venison, aged Castelmagno cheese

Feudo Croce - Primitivo di Manduria 2022

Technical Details

Producer: Feudo Croce

Region: DOC Primitivo di Manduria, Puglia, Italy

Grapes: Primitivo 100%

Vintage: 2022

Aging: 6 months in American oak barrels followed by 3 months in bottle

Alcohol: 14.5%

Serving Temperature: 16-18°C

Cellaring Potential: 2023-2028

Tasting Notes

Visual: Deep ruby red with purple highlights and significant viscosity

Nose: Rich aromatics of ripe black cherries, blackberry jam, plum preserves, vanilla, cinnamon, and chocolate with undertones of Mediterranean herbs and sweet tobacco

Palate: Full-bodied and velvety with well-integrated alcohol, moderate tannins, balanced acidity, concentrated dark fruit flavors, pleasant spice notes, and a warm, lingering finish with hints of licorice

Vintage Notes: Excellent vintage characterized by hot summer days and cool nights yielding perfectly ripened grapes with balanced sugar levels and phenolic maturity

Food Pairing: Grilled red meats, aged cheeses, hearty pasta dishes with meat sauce, spiced lamb
