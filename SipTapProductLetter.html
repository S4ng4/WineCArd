SipTap NFC Wine Card

Smart NFC & QR Wine Label – Personalized Digital Experience

Product Overview

SipTap is a smart NFC and QR-enabled tag for wine bottles, designed to instantly deliver personalized tasting notes, producer information, and storytelling with a simple tap or scan. No app required. Built for wine shops, restaurants, and boutique producers to enhance customer engagement.

Key Features

✓ Instant Tap & Scan with NFC and QR
✓ Fully customizable digital wine card
✓ Reusable and updatable anytime
✓ Durable and splash-resistant design
✓ Analytics-ready: track views and engagement
✓ Works on iOS, Android, and all major browsers

Technical Specifications

Specification Details
NFC Chip NTAG213 / NTAG216
Memory Capacity 144–868 bytes
Material Wine-safe adhesive tag
Compatibility iOS, Android, Chrome, Safari
QR Code Dynamic short link
Waterproof Yes (front label)

Instructions to Use

Attach SipTap to the wine bottle. Customers tap with NFC or scan the QR code using any smartphone. The digital wine card opens instantly in their browser. No installation needed.
