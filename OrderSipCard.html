Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SipCard packages

How do SipCards work?
SipCards use NFC (Near Field Communication) technology that works with any modern smartphone. Simply tap your phone on the card to instantly access detailed information about the wine, including tasting notes, pairings, and more.
Can I customize what appears when someone taps a SipCard?
Absolutely! All packages allow you to customize the content displayed. You can edit the AI-generated descriptions, upload your own images, and personalize the experience to match your brand or preferences.
How long do SipCards last?
SipCards are made with premium, durable materials and will last for years. The NFC technology inside is passive and doesn't require any power source, so there's no battery to replace or wear out.
What if I need more than 100 SipCards?
We offer custom enterprise solutions for larger quantities. Please contact our sales team for custom pricing and features tailored to your specific needs.
How often can I update the information?
You can update the information as often as you'd like during your subscription period. Updates are instant and don't require replacing the physical cards.
