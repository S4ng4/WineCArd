When Selling a Bottle of Wine Isn't That Simple

Working in the restaurant industry, I realized how challenging it can be to sell a bottle of wine. Even with passion and preparation, telling the story of a wine in a simple, engaging, and personalized way for each customer is a daily challenge. I often felt the frustration of knowing the value inside a bottle without being able to fully convey it to those in front of me.

From this real experience, a question was born: "What if the wine could tell its own story?"

The Idea That Sparked SipTag

That's how SipTag was born: a simple yet powerful idea. A system capable of connecting every bottle to a living story, offering an immediate and professional narrative that bridges the gap between sellers and buyers. SipTag is not just a sales tool, but also a means of education and personal enjoyment: perfect for restaurant staff seeking to enhance their wine knowledge, and for customers and enthusiasts wanting to discover wines in a free and natural way.

A Concrete Opportunity to Stand Out

For wine shops and restaurants, SipTag is a real opportunity to differentiate from competitors, offering customers a richer, more immersive experience. It's a genuine tool to increase sales, boost engagement, and highlight the hard work behind selecting and promoting great wines.

A Technology Designed for the Future

To launch SipTag and bring it quickly to the market, I chose to use NFC technology. This choice was no coincidence: I strongly believe that NFC and RFID represent the future of ultra-fast traceability, intelligent supply chain management, and may eventually replace traditional barcodes.

With a simple gesture, the customer can enjoy a fast, elegant, and captivating experience: just bring the phone close to the bottle and instantly dive into its world, discovering a new emotion with every interaction.

Just the Beginning of a Bigger Journey

SipTag is only the first step of a much bigger vision. The goal is to evolve toward a full integration with RFID and SentinelTag technologies, developing smarter, more connected solutions that transform every interaction into a moment of discovery and inspiration.

Imagine a future where every bottle, every product, and every experience can tell its own story, breaking down cultural barriers and enriching the buying experience for everyone.

SipTag: Where Wine Finds Its Voice

SipTag is not just a smart label. It's the spark of a larger vision: building a bridge between wine and people, turning every bottle into an opportunity for discovery, every tasting into a journey, and every sale into a story worth telling.
